Dum Biriyani

Inclusive of all taxes Dum Biryani is an aromatic, iconic rice dish beloved by Indians across the globe. The Haldiram's Ready-To-Eat Dum Biryani is delicately flavoured with a blend of aromatic spices, cooked with fresh vegetables and steamed basmati rice. Haldiram's Recommendation: Serve hot, topped with caramelized onions and a crunchy raita with Haldiram's Boondi on the side.
